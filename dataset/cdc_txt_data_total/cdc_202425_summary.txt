- Seasonal influenza activity remains low nationally.
- Influenza A(H1N1)pdm09, A(H3N2), and B viruses were all co-circulating during the reported week.
- Genetic and antigenic characterization of influenza viruses is ongoing.
- 1.5% of visits to healthcare providers were for respiratory illness (ILI) below the national baseline.
- No jurisdictions experienced moderate, high, or very high ILI activity during the reported week.
- FluSurv-NET reported a weekly hospitalization rate of 0.2 per 100,000 population.
- The percentage of deaths attributed to influenza remained stable compared to the previous week.
- Three influenza-associated pediatric deaths were reported during the reported week, bringing the total to 181 for the season.
- Antiviral medications are available for the treatment and prevention of influenza.
- CDC recommends annual influenza vaccination for everyone aged 6 months and older.
