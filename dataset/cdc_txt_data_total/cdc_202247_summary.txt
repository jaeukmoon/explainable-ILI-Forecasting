The key updates for Week 47, ending November 25, 2023, include an increase in seasonal influenza activity in most parts of the country, particularly in the South Central, Southeast, Mountain, and West Coast regions. In terms of virologic surveillance, there has been an increase in the percentage of respiratory specimens testing positive for influenza in clinical laboratories compared to the previous week. In outpatient respiratory illness, the percentage of visits for respiratory illness continues to be above the national baseline, with an increase in most age groups. Hospitalizations due to influenza have also increased compared to the previous week. The percentage of deaths attributed to pneumonia and influenza remains stable, while there have been 5 pediatric deaths associated with influenza reported during the current season. Antiviral susceptibility testing shows no resistance to neuraminidase inhibitors or baloxavir.
