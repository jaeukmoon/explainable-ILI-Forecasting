- Seasonal influenza activity continues to decline across the country.
- Influenza A(H3N2) viruses were the most frequently reported viruses.
- The majority of jurisdictions experienced minimal or low influenza activity.
- Hospitalization rates and P&I mortality rates are below epidemic thresholds.
- No influenza-associated pediatric deaths have been reported during the current season.
- Influenza viruses tested remain susceptible to antiviral medications.
- An annual flu vaccine is the best way to protect against influenza and its complications.
