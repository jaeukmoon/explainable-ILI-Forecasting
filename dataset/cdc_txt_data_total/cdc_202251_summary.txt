- Seasonal influenza activity is elevated and continues to increase in most parts of the country during Week 51.
- Influenza A (H1N1)pdm09 viruses have been the predominant virus circulating in all regions since Week 40.
- Outpatient visits for respiratory illness (ILI) are above baseline nationally for the eighth consecutive week and above baseline in all HHS regions.
- FluSurv-NET data shows a total of 4,697 laboratory-confirmed influenza-associated hospitalizations between October 1, 2023, and December 23, 2023.
- During Week 51, 0.5% of deaths were attributed to influenza, which increased compared to the previous week.
- Seven influenza-associated pediatric deaths were reported during Week 51, bringing the total for the 2023-2024 season to 20.
- CDC estimates that there have been at least 7.1 million influenza illnesses, 73,000 hospitalizations, and 4,500 deaths so far this season.
