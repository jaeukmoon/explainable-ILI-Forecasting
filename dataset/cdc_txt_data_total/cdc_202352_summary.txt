1. Seasonal influenza activity is elevated and continues to increase in most parts of the country.
2. Influenza A viruses, including H3N2 and H1N1pdm09, predominate in laboratory-tested specimens.
3. Outpatient visits for respiratory illness remain above the national baseline, with 6.9% of visits attributed to influenza-like illness (ILI).
4. Louisiana experienced moderate ILI activity, while the majority of jurisdictions reported minimal ILI activity.
5. Four out of 53 jurisdictions reported local flu activity, while the remainder reported sporadic or no flu activity.
6. Hospitalizations and mortality rates related to influenza are updated weekly.
7. No influenza-associated pediatric deaths have been reported during the current season.
