- **Outpatient respiratory illness visits**: 2.1% of outpatient visits were for ILI, below the 2.5% national baseline but showing gradual increases in recent weeks, particularly among ages 0-24.

- **Expectation of flu activity from CDC**: Flu activity remains low but is gradually increasing. Increases among children and young adults suggest future elevated activity, primarily by H3N2.

- **Other key factors**: Long-term care facilities reported 0.2% of residents with influenza-positive tests. Activity levels varied geographically, with only one state reporting high activity, 44 states reporting minimal levels, and others classified as moderate or low.
