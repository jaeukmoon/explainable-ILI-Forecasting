- Seasonal influenza activity remains low nationally.
- Influenza A(H1N1)pdm09, A(H3N2), and B viruses are all co-circulating.
- Genetic and antigenic characterization of influenza viruses is ongoing.
- The percentage of visits for respiratory illness remains below the national baseline.
- ILI activity varies by location, with no jurisdictions experiencing moderate or high activity.
- The hospitalization rate for laboratory-confirmed influenza is 0.1 per 100,000 population.
- The percentage of deaths attributed to influenza is stable and below the epidemic threshold.
- Three influenza-associated pediatric deaths were reported this week, bringing the total for the season to 178 deaths.
- Vaccination and antiviral drugs are recommended for prevention and treatment of influenza.
- The CDC provides additional national and international influenza surveillance information through various sources.
