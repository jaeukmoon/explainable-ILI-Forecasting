Week37, 2021
1. Nationwide influenza activity remains low, with 2.1% of outpatient visits for ILI, below the national baseline of 2.6%.
2. One case of human infection with a variant Influenza A(H1N2)v virus was reported in Ohio, marking the third A(H1N2)v case this year. Limited human-to-human transmission cannot be ruled out.
3. 21.1% of deaths were due to pneumonia, influenza, and/or COVID-19 (PIC), primarily attributed to COVID-19, exceeding the epidemic threshold of 5.5%.
4. No influenza-associated pediatric deaths were reported for week 37; one pediatric death has occurred this season.
5. Influenza virus circulation remains low, and recent increases in ILI visits are attributed to other respiratory viruses.
6. Data from clinical laboratories indicate limited influenza transmission, with lower positivity rates.
