- CDC is preparing for co-circulating influenza virus, SARS-CoV-2, and respiratory syncytial virus (RSV) in the upcoming fall and winter seasons.
- Vaccines are essential in providing protection against all three respiratory diseases, especially for older adults who are at a higher risk of severe illness.
- Clinicians play a crucial role in encouraging older adults to stay up to date on influenza, COVID-19, and RSV vaccinations.
- The webinar will provide updates on the latest recommendations and clinical considerations for administering these vaccines to adults 60 years and older.
- Resources and communication strategies will be discussed to facilitate older adult vaccination.
