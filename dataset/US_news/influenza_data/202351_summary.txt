Vaccination Coverage: As of December 9, 2023, approximately 42.2% of adults aged 18 years and older had received the influenza vaccine and 18.3% had received the updated 2023-2024 COVID-19 vaccine. Additionally, 17.0% of adults aged 60 years and older had received the RSV vaccine.

Openness to Vaccination: Many adults who had not yet received the vaccines reported being open to vaccination, with approximately 27% of adults aged 18 years and older and 53% of adults aged 60 years and older expressing definite or probable intention to be vaccinated or uncertain about vaccination against influenza, COVID-19, and RSV.

Impact of Provider Recommendations: Strong recommendations and offers of vaccination from healthcare providers can potentially increase vaccination coverage for influenza, COVID-19, and RSV.

Vaccination Patterns and Attitudes: Immunization programs and vaccination partners are encouraged to use data on vaccination patterns and attitudes towards vaccination in their jurisdictions to inform planning, implementation, strengthening, and evaluation of vaccination efforts.
