- Avian influenza, or bird flu, is a topic of concern that requires effective communication to the public.
- The CDC has developed a social media toolkit to help partners in communicating about avian influenza.
- The toolkit provides resources and materials to educate the public about avian influenza, its prevention, and current situation.
- The toolkit aims to dispel myths and provide accurate information about avian influenza.
- It includes videos, case definitions for investigations, clinical guidance, public health strategies, and information on testing and diagnosis.
- The toolkit is available in multiple languages to reach a wider audience.
- The CDC emphasizes the importance of effective communication to prevent the spread of avian influenza and protect public health.
