Avian Influenza (Bird Flu): CDC is currently monitoring the situation of H5N1 bird flu cases in California. There have been a total of six cases reported in the state. Genetic sequencing data of H5N1 bird flu viruses from California has been shared by CDC. A pilot program has been initiated to test symptomatic farm and dairy workers for H5 bird flu. CDC is also providing free seasonal flu vaccines to farm workers in states affected by H5N1 bird flu.
