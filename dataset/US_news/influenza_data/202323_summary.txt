- The Pan American Health Organization/World Health Organization (PAHO/WHO) recommends strengthening surveillance for influenza, respiratory syncytial virus (RSV), and SARS-CoV-2, and adopting necessary prevention and control measures for severe outcomes.
- The increase in respiratory viruses activity, including hospitalizations among children under 2 years old, in the Andean sub region and Southern cone, has been observed.
- Measures for prevention and control include ensuring high vaccination coverage in high-risk groups, appropriate clinical management, strict compliance with infection prevention control measures, and timely treatment of associated complications.
- It is crucial to integrate surveillance for these respiratory viruses and strengthen healthcare services and supplies of antivirals.
