Such minimal influenza activity aligns with the observed decline in ILI cases and the low future count.

   Ongoing impacts from the COVID-19 pandemic were noted heavily in the reports, with altered healthcare-seeking behaviors and disrupted surveillance systems complicating data interpretation (all weeks). These pandemic-driven factors likely contributed to an underrepresentation of traditional ILI activity and its continuation into the future.
