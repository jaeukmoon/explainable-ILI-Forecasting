The prediction of 1910 future ILI occurrences after 5 weeks is a result of analyzing trends in the time-series data and considering the low influenza activity reported in the weekly surveillance reports. Although seasonal influenza activity remains unusually low (e.g., only 0.1% of specimens testing positive and ILI visits consistently below the national baseline of 2.6%), recent weeks show a gradual upward trend in the time-series data (e.g., occurrences increased from 880 to 1054 in the last few weeks).
